This model was trained using Stanford Dogs Dataset which has 20,580 pictures of 120 kinds of dogs! (That's a lot, isn't it?)
Its creators reached an accuracy of about 22% while using 100 images in each training class. I have reached about 95% of accuracy, using a Deep Neural Network! The dataset includes photos of only 120 breeds.Therefore, this program can accurately predict your dog's breed only if it is in the dataset. Essentially, the model selects the breed the dog in your photo most closely resembles from the breeds it has seen before.

This is the first version of this model. In the second version, I will find photos of all recognized breeds and train my model on them!

But so far, for the best results:

  • The breed of the dog should be pure
  • The dog better be fully depicted in the image (Although it works even with parts of the dog in the picture)
  • The photo better be well-lit
  • Nothing should come between the camera and the dog

FAQ

Question: Why does it take so long to predict the breed?

Answer: As I am trying to keep my expenses at a minimum, I have this hosting with relatively weak computing power, without GPU support. In real applications, prediction is instant.

Question: Is it safe to upload photos?

Answer: Absolutely! I use the TSL protocol that protects your photos on the Internet. Also, after making a prediction, the server instantly deletes photos, and I do not receive/see/use them for training my Version 2 network.

Question: Can it Predict Mixed Breeds?

Answer: It prediscovers only purebreds, but if at least one of the breeds that make up the mix of a specific dog is present in the dataset, my model will likely predict it correctly.

Question: Can it Predict Cats?

Answer: No, it cannot predict cats, birds, flowers, trees, etc. But if you have an idea for a useful application, please get in touch with me via the form on my website.

Question: Why it did not predict my dog correctly?

F1-score is like a game score that shows how well a model makes correct predictions and avoids mistakes at the same time.
